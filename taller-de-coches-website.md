# Taller de Coches — Website Strategy & Content Guide

---

## Brand Voice

| Attribute | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Tone** | Professional but approachable. Like a master mechanic explaining a problem to a friend. |
| **Attitude** | No-nonsense, direct, humble confidence. We know our trade — we don't need to oversell it. |
| **Language** | Short sentences. Active voice. Plain Spanish or Catalan (no Spanglish jargon). Use the language your customers actually speak in Arbós. |
| **What we avoid** | "Premium experience," "unparalleled service," "state-of-the-art," "cutting-edge," "holistic approach," "seamless," "optimize," "solutions." |
| **What we use instead** | "Arreglemos cotxes." "Trobem el problema." "Et donem una resposta clara." "Portem 20 anys a Arbós." |
| **Emotional register** | Reassuring, grounded. Car trouble is stressful — we make it less stressful by being honest and competent. |


5 pages. No bloat. No blog unless you actually write it monthly.

### DIAGNOSTICS PAGE (Secret weapon — shows technical credibility)

**Headline:**
> No adivinamos. Diagnosticamos.

**Body:**
> Tenemos un equipo de diagnosis multimarca que lee la centralita de tu coche como si fuera un libro abierto. Vemos los códigos de error en directo, comprobamos sensores, presiones, temperaturas — todo antes de tocar una sola pieza.

**Bullets:**
- Lectura OBD2 avanzada (multimarca: BMW, Audi, SEAT, Renault, Toyota, Mercedes, etc.)
- Comprobación de sensores en tiempo real (ABS, airbag, inyección, turbocompresor)
- Osciloscopio automotriz para fallos intermitentes
- Sin recambios inútiles — solo reparamos lo que está roto

**Tone:**
> Algunos talleres cambian piezas hasta que el problema desaparece. Nosotros preferimos saber qué está pasando antes de meter mano. Te ahorra tiempo y dinero.

## UX / UI Recommendations

### Layout priorities (mobile-first)

1. **Sticky header:** Phone number + "Pedir presupuesto" button. Always visible.
2. **Hero:** Big photo of the actual shop (not a generic garage photo). Headline overlay. One CTA button.
3. **Trust bar:** "20 años en Arbós · Diagnóstico gratuito · Presupuesto sin compromiso"
4. **Services:** 3-column grid on desktop, single column on mobile. No icons — use real photos.
5. **Why choose us:** 3 cards, no icons, short text.
6. **Google Maps embed + hours:** Footer-level.
7. **Footer:** Phone, WhatsApp, address, hours. That's it.

### Color palette

| Role | Hex | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary background** | #1A1A1A (near-black) | Industrial, serious. Feels like a real shop floor. |
| **Primary text** | #F5F5F5 (off-white) | Easier on the eyes than pure white. |
| **Accent** | #E63946 (red) | Urgency for CTAs. Matches tool handles, brake lights. |
| **Secondary accent** | #457B9D (steel blue) | Technical, trustworthy. Used for diagnosis page. |
| **Warm accent** | #D4A373 (worn leather/copper) | Human touch. Used for hover states, borders, subtle dividers. |

**Why this palette works:**
No pastels. No corporate blues (IBM look). The near-black + red + leather tone feels like an actual garage — not a SaaS company.

### Typography

- **Headings:** A bold sans-serif like Inter Black or Montserrat Black. Heavy, confident.
- **Body:** A clean, readable sans-serif like Work Sans or IBM Plex Sans. No serifs — they look old in a bad way on auto sites.
- **Size:** 16px minimum body. No tiny text.

### CTA button copy

| Context | Button text |
|---|---|
| Hero | "Llama ahora" |
| Sticky header | "Pedir presupuesto" |
| Services page | "Pregúntanos" |
| Contact page | "Enviar consulta" |

**Never:** "Get started," "Book now," "Schedule appointment." Keep it human.

## Human-Touch Features (The Anti-AI Checklist)

### 1. Real photos or nothing
No generated images. No generic "mechanic smiling at camera" stock photo. The shop floor, the tools, the team, the cars. Grainy is fine. Dark is fine. Real is the only rule.

### 2. Name the mechanics
> "Habla con Yusef o Karim — ellos llevan cada coche como si fuera suyo."

Adding real first names to the copy creates instant trust. If there are only 2-3 mechanics, list them.

### 3. A specific local reference
> "Estamos a la entrada de Arbós, Santa Lucía 1 — al lado del campo de fútbol. No tiene pérdida."

This single sentence does more for trust than any "We are committed to excellence" paragraph ever could.

### 4. Include the shop dog (if there is one)
Or the cat. Or the plant. Whatever lives in the shop. It's free personality.

### 5. "We don't do what we can't fix"
A small honesty statement:
> "Si no podemos repararlo, te lo decimos. Preferimos perder un cliente a engañarlo."

### 6. Anecdote on the diagnostic page
> "La semana pasada entró un Clío que llevaba tres meses en otro taller. Le habían cambiado la batería, el alternador y el motor de arranque. Nosotros lo conectamos al diagnóstico y en 10 minutos vimos que era un sensor de masa de aire. Se fue en una hora."

This kind of specific, real story is the most powerful copy you can write. No AI can invent a good one — you need to ask the actual mechanics for their war stories.

### 7. Handwritten-style note (optional)
A scan of an actual handwritten note from the owner:
> "Gracias por confiar en nosotros. — Yusef"

Placed at the bottom of the homepage. Pixelated, crooked, perfect.

### 8. What NOT to do (De-AI-ifying checklist)

| Bad | Good |
|---|---|
| "Your vehicle deserves the best care" | "Tu coche necesita un buen mecánico. Eso somos." |
| "State-of-the-art diagnostic equipment" | "Tenemos una máquina que lee todas las centralitas." |
| "Comprehensive automotive solutions" | "Arreglamos coches. Motor, chapa, pintura, luces." |
| "Book your appointment today" | "Pásate o llama. Sin cita previa." |
| Icons for services | Photos of actual work |
| Gradient backgrounds | Solid, flat, no-nonsense |
| Testimonials with full names and photos | One good quote from a neighbor, with permission |
| "We put the customer first" | We don't say it. We just answer the phone. |

## Developer Notes

| Requirement | Instruction |
|---|---|
| **Framework** | Plain HTML/CSS/JS or a lightweight static site. No React for a 5-page site. |
| **Fonts** | Google Fonts: Inter (headings) + Work Sans (body). Self-hosted fallback. |
| **Maps** | Google Maps embed with a custom pin (red, to match accent). |
| **Photos** | WebP format. Max 200KB each. Lazy load below the fold. |
| **CTA tracking** | Google Tag Manager + a phone number click event. Simple. |
| **Mobile** | The hero CTA must be tappable with one thumb. No tiny buttons. |
| **Load time** | Under 2s. No unnecessary JS. No heavy animations. |
| **Hosting** | Netlify or Vercel. Custom domain. Free SSL. |

---

## Summary: The 3 Non-Negotiables

1. **Real photos.** If you don't have them, the site will look fake. Spend a morning taking them.
2. **A local reference in the copy.** Street, landmark, neighborhood — ground it in one place.
3. **Straight talk.** Every sentence should pass the "would a mechanic actually say this?" test.
